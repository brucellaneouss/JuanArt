About Me

Hi! I'm Bruce Casiano, a 20-year-old artist passionate about creating expressive artworks and paintings. Art has always been my way to connect with others and bring my ideas to life. With each piece, I aim to share a bit of my journey, inspiration, and the things I love most. Welcome to my world of creativity—I'm excited to share it with you!

How I Started?

My art journey began during the pandemic, in May 2020. With time at home, I experimented with different pencil shades and colored pencils, discovering how each could bring my ideas to life in unique ways. Now, I’m committed to growing my craft and sharing my work with others.

Commissions

A few months after refining my skills, I began taking on commissions. It was an exciting way to turn my passion into income, connect with new people, and challenge myself creatively. Each project has helped me grow as an artist, and I’m grateful for the support and trust my clients have shown along the way.

Services Offered

Art is personal, and I enjoy creating pieces that resonate with each client. From detailed portraits to expressive murals, I bring a variety of styles to meet different needs. My services include:

Graphite Pencils Drawing

Classic black-and-white illustrations that emphasize light, shadow, and fine details. Perfect for capturing realistic portraits or intricate designs.

Colored Pencils Art

Vibrant, textured works that add depth and personality to each subject. Ideal for lively portraits, nature scenes, or any piece that calls for a pop of color.

Canvas Paintings

Custom paintings on canvas, ranging from lifelike depictions to abstract art. It adds a personal touch to any space and make great statement pieces.

Wall Paintings

Large-scale murals and wall art designed to transform any environment. These pieces are perfect for adding creativity and character to your home or business.
